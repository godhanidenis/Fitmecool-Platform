Account Deletion and Data Removal

At FitMeCool, we value your privacy and provide a straightforward process for account deletion. Follow these steps to request the removal of your account:

Step 1: Opent the App

Open the app and login to your account if not logged In.

Step 2: Locate Account Deletion Option

Open the drawer by clicking on the drawer icon situated on the top left corner of the screen, where you find the option "Delete Account."

Step 3: Confirm Deletion

Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm your decision. Be aware that this action is irreversible.

Data Removal: What You Need to Know

We take data privacy seriously. When you request account deletion, the following types of data will be removed:

  1. Profile Information: Your name, username, and any other personal details associated with your account.
  2. Uploaded Products: All the details of your uploaded products if any will get removed.
  3. Messages and Communication: Any messages or communication history within the app will be deleted.
  4. Wishlist and Favorites: Information related to your saved items or favorites will be removed.
  5. Login Credentials: Your login credentials, including encrypted passwords, will be deactivated.

Note: It may take some time for the removal process to complete, and residual data may remain in system backups for a limited duration.

For any further assistance or inquiries related to account deletion, please contact our support team at fot.appdev@gmail.com.
